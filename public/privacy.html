Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

Introduction

AmakaFlow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("AmakaFlow Companion") and web application (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Profile information you choose to provide

Workout Data

We collect workout-related information including:

  • Workout structures and exercise data you create or import
  • Device preferences and settings
  • Workout completion records and performance data

Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version and usage statistics

Device Permissions

We only request permissions that are essential for app functionality.

Camera Permission

The camera is used solely for QR code scanning to pair your mobile device with the AmakaFlow web application. This enables seamless workout transfer between devices. Camera access is:

  • Only activated when you initiate QR code scanning
  • Never used to capture photos or videos
  • Never used for facial recognition or any other purpose
  • Optional - you can manually enter pairing codes instead

Microphone Permission (Optional)

If enabled, microphone access is used only for voice commands during workout sessions. This feature is entirely optional and can be disabled at any time.

Vibration Permission

Used to provide haptic feedback during workouts, such as timer alerts and rest period notifications.

Internet Access

Required to sync your workouts across devices and access our cloud services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Sync your workouts across devices
  • Enable workout export to fitness devices (Garmin, Wahoo, etc.)
  • Send you service-related notifications
  • Respond to your requests and support inquiries
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our Services

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure (Supabase)
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly review and update our security measures

Third-Party Services

We may share data with the following categories of third-party services:

Authentication Providers

We use Clerk for secure user authentication. Their privacy policy can be found at clerk.com/privacy.

Cloud Infrastructure

We use Supabase for data storage and backend services. Their privacy policy can be found at supabase.com/privacy.

Fitness Device Integrations

If you choose to connect third-party fitness services (such as Strava or Garmin Connect), we will share workout data as necessary to enable these integrations. Each integration is optional and can be disconnected at any time.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Download your workout data in portable formats
  • Opt-out: Disable optional features like voice commands

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as resolving disputes).

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
